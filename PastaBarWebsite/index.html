About us

Founded in 1989 in Montreal on Chabanel Street, Pasta Bar is a family-style Italian restaurant that has stood the test of time. We take pride in serving dishes made with the highest quality ingredients and products. With a flair that doesn’t compromise on quality, authenticity, or taste, we prepare fresh meats, fish, poultry, vegetables, salads, soups, and, of course, traditional pasta dishes every day.

Recognized as one of the best authentic Italian restaurants and caterers, Pasta Bar boasts a rich history behind each savory dish. We invite you to enjoy a homemade meal and embark on a wonderful culinary journey with us. Buon appetito!
